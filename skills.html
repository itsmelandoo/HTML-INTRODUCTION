Communication Skills

Active Listening

Active listening is an engaged and responsive way of hearing and understanding someone’s message. It involves fully concentrating on the speaker, providing feedback through verbal acknowledgments and non-verbal cues like nodding, and reflecting on what has been said. By clarifying and summarizing key points, active listening ensures that the speaker feels heard and understood, fostering effective communication and building stronger relationships. This process helps avoid misunderstandings and shows respect for the speaker’s perspective.

Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence is the ability to recognize, understand, and manage one’s own emotions, as well as to empathize with and navigate the emotions of others.

Feedback

Feedback is constructive information or advice provided to someone to help them improve their performance or behavior.
